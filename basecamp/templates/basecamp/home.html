Popular questions

The price you get from EasyGo is included airport parking fee. Even if your flight is delayed, we do not charge anything for the waiting time occurred. Our price is fixed. there is no extra charge.

EasyGo drivers will keep track of your flights landing time in order to meet you on time. He will call or message you by the time you collect your baggage. All you have to do is that you can go to the meeting point. He will be waiting for you there.

EasyGo accepts all major Credit Cards, we accept American Express, MasterCard, VisaCard. or you can simply pay cash to your driver on the booking day

Yes we do provide a infant seat or baby capsule (one car seat is free of charge but we charge extra $10 more than two seats. it has to be mentioned on the booking process)

Thirteen (13) passengers fit in a standard shuttle mini bus. you can check the photo of the vehicle on the webpage 'EASYGO SERVICES'

EASYGO permits one (1) bag plus one (1) carry-on per person. If you have additional bags, please inform our office.
